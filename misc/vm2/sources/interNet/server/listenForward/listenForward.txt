packet layout: byte:type, word:connection, msbword:sizeofData, bytes:data
types are:
0 - CS - discard...
1 - CS - echo request...
2 - CS - echo reply...
3 - S  - open connection; 16bytes:addr, msbword:port...
4 - C  - accept connection; 16bytes:addr, msbword:port...
5 - CS - close connection...
6 - CS - send data...
