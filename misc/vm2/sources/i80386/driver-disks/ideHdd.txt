block mode pipeline should opened for this process...
after the pipeline has opened, one dword is sent
to the caller... this dword is the number of drives
available... the caller should reply to it by the number
of the drive interested in... this number is a dword
and must be in the range 0..max-1...
after this handshake, the protocol is the following:

the caller send a packet to this process... the first
dword is the command word, the others are the data...
this process replies with a packet, in witch, the
first dword is the status word, the others are data...

the commands are the following:

00 - identify drive... (hd/cd)
     in: none...
     out: dw:port, db:device, db: lba, dd:cyl,hed,sec, dd:begin,size,
          str:model, str:serial, str:firm...

01 - read one sector... (hd)
     in:  dd:secnum (0..max-1)...
     out: dd:secnum, 512:data...

02 - write one sector... (hd)
     in:  dd:secnum (0..max-1), 512:data...
     out: dd:secnum...

03 - (un)lock door... (cd)
     in:  dd:status; 0=free, 1=lock...
     out: nothing....

04 - start playback... (cd)
     in:  dd:first, dd:last...
     out: nothing....

05 - pause playback... (cd)
     in:  dd:status; 0=resume, 1=pause...
     out: nothing....

06 - stop playback... (cd)
     in:  nothing....
     out: nothing....

07 - open/close door... (cd)
     in:  dd:status; 0=close, 1=open...
     out: nothing....

08 - read subchannel data... (cd)
     in:  nothing....
     out: dd:trackNumber(-1=none), dd: positionInTrack...

09 - disk readyness test... (cd)
     in:  nothing....
     out: dd:status; 0=no, 1=yes...

10 - read one toc entry... (cd)
     in:  dd: trackNumber (min..max, 0=header)
     out: dd:firstTrack,lastTrack, dd:trackNumber, dd:beginning,ending
          dd:type; 10h..13h=audio, 14h..17h=data...

11 - read one sector... (cd)
     in:  dd: secNum (0..max-1)
     out: dd:secnum, 2048:data...
